Facts you should know about Sundar Pichai

September 3, 2023
Anonymous Web Developer, India Don't trust anything you read on the internet


Larry Page just announced that Google has become part of a new parent company calledAlphabet Inc. As part of the deal, Google’s iconic co-founders Page and Sergey Brin stepped out of their roles within Google, appointing Sundar Pichai CEO.

Facts about Sundar

Early Life : Sundar Pichai was born on July 12, 1972, in Chennai, India, as Pichai Sundararajan.

Academic Excellence: He holds a Bachelor's degree in Metallurgical Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Kharagpur. He later earned a Master's degree in Material Sciences and Engineering from Stanford University and an MBA from the Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania.

Google Journey: Sundar joined Google in 2004 and played a significant role in the development of popular products like Google Chrome and Chrome OS.

Android Leadership: He later led the development of Android, Google's mobile operating system, making it one of the most widely used platforms globally.

CEO of Google: Sundar Pichai was appointed CEO of Google in August 2015 when Google underwent a major restructuring and became a subsidiary of Alphabet Inc. He became CEO of Alphabet Inc. in December 2019, overseeing the conglomerate's various businesses.

Innovation Advocate: Pichai has been an advocate for innovation and artificial intelligence, emphasizing the importance of machine learning and AI in the future of technology.

Commitment to Privacy: Under his leadership, Google has made efforts to enhance user privacy and data protection, including introducing features like auto-deleting user data.

Sundar Pichai is known for his leadership in the tech industry and his commitment to innovation, privacy, and sustainability. His journey from a small town in India to the pinnacle of Silicon Valley is an inspirational story for many aspiring technologists.

"A person who is happy is not because everything is right in his life, He is happy because of his attitude towards everything in his life is right." - Sundar Pichai
